BURGER

Description

Experience the magic of one of the tasties creations mankind ever served on a plate!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Buns
  • Condements
  • Salad

Steps

  1. Cook the beef on the hot pan
  2. Season with salt and pepper
  3. Cut the bun in half and add condements such as ketchup and mayo
  4. Stack up the beef and salad into the bun
  5. Take a bite
