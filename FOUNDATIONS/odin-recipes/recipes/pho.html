PHO

Description

Experience the magic of one of the greatest noodle soups in the world with this easy to follow traditional Vietnamese Pho recipe!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Salt and pepper
  • Beef
  • Noodles
  • Spring Onions

Steps

  1. Cook the beef in the water to make the broth
  2. Season the broof to your liking
  3. Take the beef out and cut it into slices
  4. Add the noodles into the boiling broth
  5. Serve the broof with the noodles and top it with the chopped spring onions and beef slices
